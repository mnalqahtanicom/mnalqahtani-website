# Strategy to Results™ — Framework (one page)

Strategy → Execution → Change → Adoption → Performance → Results

1. Strategy   — A clear, ownable direction.
2. Execution  — Disciplined, governed delivery.
3. Change     — Leading people through the change.
4. Adoption   — Embedding new ways of working.
5. Performance— Measuring what matters.
6. Results    — Sustained, measurable impact.

Ask at each stage: What does "done" look like, who owns it, and how will we know?
